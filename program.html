Program

11.09–17.11.2019

Curatorial Exhibition

Nine exhibitors, three Estonian and six from around the world, have been chosen for their diverse palette of agendas and aesthetics; together they exhibit a collective pluralism and demonstrate that beauty matters.

12.09–13.09.2019

Conference Programme: Symposium

The 2-day symposium is geared for architects of all ages, from Estonia and abroad, and for the general public, who particularly interested in culture, and wish to catch up with the now in various disciplines.

12.09–29.09.2019

Vision Competition Exhibition

Vision Competition “New Habitats, New Beauties” was related to the main curatorial topic “Beauty Matters” finding alternative lifestyles of Tallinn inhabitants. The exhibition will showcase the competition entries.

12.09–17.11.2019

Installation Programme Exhibition

The exhibition will showcase the models of the 12 finalists of TAB Installation Programme Competition, which took place from 2nd November until 30th of January 2019 in the Museum of Estonian Architecture.

11.09–09.10.2019

International School’s Exhibition

TAB International Architecture Schools‘ Exhibition “Terribly Beautiful” is part of TAB Main Programme and includes the latest work of academic institutions that teach architecture in various parts of the world.

11.09–30.09.2019

Satellite Programme

TAB 2019 Satellite Programme offers an opportunity to showcase accompanying exhibitions and creative ideas in various locations in the heart of Tallinn during Tallinn Architecture Biennale TAB 2019.

11.09–11.09.2019

Conference Programme: "Space and Digital Reality"

We believe that Digital Reality is having a major impact on space and architecture. And we want to research, imagine and speculate about the future of architectural creation, urban development as well as design education with our visitors.

11.09–15.09.2019

TAB- Club

TAB- Club is the centre of the biennale, an information hub and a meeting place which excites equally any urbanite or a passer-by with movie nights, workshops, open talks and parties.

Headline Sponsor

TAB is the regions biggest architecture and urban planning festival engaging the public and the profession in the conversation around architecture and our city.
