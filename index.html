Vision Competition winners are known

TAB Vision Competition “New Habitats, New Beauties” received more than 80 proposals from architects and architecture students from all over the world. The 1st prize was awarded to the entry by the name Lines Rehab, followed by 2nd prize Voxel Valley and 3rd place Eatable.

TAB Satellite Programme is open

We are looking for exhibitions, installations, happenings. The idea can be presented as an exhibition or a 10 minute performance, as a music or dance piece or a scientific experiment, a workshop or an excursion connected to this year’s Biennale’s topic "Beauty Matters".

Overall results of Installation Programme Competition

The winning project Steampunk is a proposal for a pavilion made of steam-bent timber elements, using analogue tools augmented with the precision of mixed reality environments. The Installation will open to the public during TAB 2019 Opening Week on September 11th, 2019.
